Who I Am

My name is Remy. I'm a web designer and developer based in Enugu, Nigeria.

I am excited and passionate about what I do and really talented when it comes to being ingenious and creating stuff. Ever since I was a small boy, I have always been captivated by the wonders of technology.

I could remember having a toy car with wired remote control, the remote control could only move the car back and front and I thought to myself, How do I make this car move sideways like a real car? How do I get rid of the remote wire connected to the car and still maintain a connection? Those thoughts were my rebirth to the marvels of technology and what I can create from it.

You can always trust me to give my absolute best and I look forward to helping individuals and companies create a web presence
